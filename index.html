About me

My professional background is in science and written content. After earning a Bachelor of Science in chemistry and biology from Victoria University of Wellington, I worked in an analytical chemistry lab and as a research assistant helping to research biodiesel formulations. I also worked as a content writer for blogs and websites. Eventually, this became my full time job. I always worked alongside web developers, which drove my interest in enrolling at Enspiral DevAcademy. I look forward to a future in which I can bring all of my skills to the fore creating websites.
